Minial Writer is a responsive WordPress blog and news theme made for bloggers and newspapers. Its has an dark elegant and minimalistic look make its an attractive theme for writers who wish to write about their view on food, fashion, travel, clothing, movies or their lifestyle in general. Minimalistblogger is of course modern in the ways that it is both fast and search engine optimized (SEO friendly) as a theme should be nowadays. This is the black version of the white Minimalistblogger, it comes with a bunch of new features and advertisement opportunities. The sidebar allows widgets, adsense affiliate links or whatever else you wish to put there.
